## Book Dimensions B1–B6

| Dim | Score | One-line reason |
|-----|-------|-----------------|
| **B1 Macro Structure（卷级结构）** | **8** | Two volumes both load-bearing and escalating: V1《测绘》records the outside (shell, register, buried debt); V2《没顶》is the loss recording can't stop. The 点云 / 名册 split is a single causal architecture, and the macro shape itself enacts the theme (record → submersion). Held back from 9 only by the book's small 2-vol scale — the "sequence of volume arcs" has just one hinge to carry meaning through. |
| **B2 Promise Payoff（伏笔兑现）** | **9** | Every plant pays, and payoffs braid: 铜牌「浔渡贰号」→ 用途 filled with 渡; the 名册 空条 + 「江」姓 fake-out → resolved by subversion (her family was all on the boat; the missing are the Zhong household); 「照得住名字么」→ answered by the whole book; 正梁那片补不上的黑 → the one thing the machine can't cover, sinks with the real beam while the raw un-补 file is kept; 「东西送到了得有人签收」→ she becomes签收人. In hindsight none looked like plants. |
| **B3 Character Continuity（人物长线）** | **8** | 江照's turn — from "记录就能留住" to taking on the human way of keeping — is cumulative and each step costs (取消轮岗, 交底, 签收, 带名过水). 钟阿婆 stays consistent and gets the un-sentimental exit (leaves for her niece's rather than 殉谷). 周晏's 「了了」/「到了」register never breaks. Small cast makes continuity easier, but the distance traveled is genuinely felt. |
| **B4 Tension Curve（张力曲线）** | **8** | Tension is moral/internal by design and deepens rather than inflates: the black spot → the register → the family-debt reveal → the crossing. The quietest, latest chapter (011, the ferry) carries the book's highest charge — exactly the healthy shape. Docked from 9 because amplitude range is deliberately narrow; a reader wanting escalation of event will feel the contemplative register as flat in the unsampled middle. |
| **B5 Cast Management（阵容管理）** | **8** | Small, fully controlled cast (江照 / 钟阿婆 / 周晏, with offstage 江守、三叔、陆、钟满). Introductions staged cleanly, exits explicit and remembered, zero blur or unexplained vanishing. The social world (老浔口, the migrants, the 移民普查) feels larger than the pages. Minimal by design, so the dimension's "breathing large system" ceiling isn't reachable. |
| **B6 Resolution（收束）** | **9** | The ending pays the premise's oldest debt. The premise question — what does「一比一、分毫不差」actually hold? — is answered by the person 江照 became: not by rescue (「命是捞不回来的，锁死在五十三年前」) but by becoming the human 签收人, carrying the name across water, and filling 钟满's five-decade-empty 葬处 with a 1:1 coordinate — the surveyor's own instrument turned to hold a person instead of a shell, and 用途 = 渡. Unguessable from page one, inevitable in hindsight, feeling persists past the last line. |
| **Total** | **50/60** | Strong, coherent literary book; no sagging middle in sampled evidence, no reset, no contradiction. |

## Must Fix (book-level structural / resolution hard defects)

**None.** Continuity is clean across the sampled span; the 「江」姓 fake-out is resolved without contradiction; the ending discharges the premise-debt rather than the last volume's only.

## Should

- **S1 (unsampled-middle tension):** Confirm vol2 chapters 004–010/012 (not in packet) sustain charge; the narrow, contemplative amplitude that works at the climax is the one place a mid-book sag could hide. Sampled evidence gives no sign of it, but it's the only structural risk worth a targeted read.
- **S2 (scale ceiling, not a defect):** B1/B4/B5 sit at 8 partly because the 2-vol / 12-ch scale caps what "volume-arc sequence" and "cast as system" can express. This is a size fact, not a flaw — flagged so the score isn't mistaken for weak execution.

---

## Verdict for the book

As a book, the resolution is **worth its premise** — and then some: it takes「一比一、分毫不差」at face value only to prove that the one datum that matters (a name, a person's 下落) is exactly what the 1:1 shell can't hold, then has the protagonist fill that gap with a 1:1 coordinate used for the opposite of the project's intent. The book pays for the ending it was always paying for.
